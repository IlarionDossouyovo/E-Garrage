✅ Use Orbitron for headings  
✅ Keep dark background  
✅ Add glow effects for emphasis  
✅ Use "E" with gradient  

### Don'ts

❌ Mix with other blue shades  
❌ Use plain fonts  
❌ Add too many colors  
❌ Use on cluttered backgrounds
